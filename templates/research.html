Research at Hazara University

Hazara University promotes innovation and knowledge across all faculties through advanced research, interdisciplinary collaboration, and industry engagement.

Key Research Areas:

  • Environmental and Climate Change Studies
  • Biotechnology and Life Sciences
  • Heritage and Cultural Preservation
  • Data Science and AI
  • Social Sciences and Policy Development

Research Centers:

  • Center for Climate & Environmental Research
  • Bioinformatics & Molecular Genetics Lab
  • HU Archaeology and Cultural Studies Center
  • AI & Data Science Research Cluster

For funding opportunities, conferences, and thesis support, contact the university’s research office or visit the official website.
